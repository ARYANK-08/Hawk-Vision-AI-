About Us

Experience true independence with our app designed for visually impaired individuals. It scans and recognizes objects in the surroundings, providing comprehensive situational awareness. Navigate confidently and make informed decisions with enhanced awareness and freedom.
Object Detection

Our innovative system offers real-time object detection through a camera, providing immediate audio output to assist visually impaired individuals. By simply pointing the camera at an object, users receive valuable information audibly, including the object's name, precise position, and distance. With voice commands, users can effortlessly interact with the system, requesting information about their surroundings. This technology revolutionizes accessibility, empowering visually impaired individuals to navigate their environment .

read text

Our cutting-edge system utilizes real-time text detection capabilities, enabling users to simply point the camera at any text document, newspaper, or book, and receive an audio output that reads the contents aloud. This technology provides visually impaired individuals with immediate access to written information. By capturing and processing text in real-time, the system accurately recognizes and converts it into spoken words, ensuring an effortless and seamless reading experience.

AR NAVIGATION

Our advanced system uses augmented reality to guide visually impaired individuals to their desired destinations. It overlays digital information onto the real-world environment, creating a guided path with visual and auditory cues. A sound checkpoint feature ensures correct navigation by providing audio feedback at specific intervals. If the user deviates from the path, the system immediately corrects them with audio instructions

CUrrency detection

Our innovative system enables visually impaired individuals to easily determine the amount of money they receive during cash transactions. By pointing the camera at a banknote, the system generates a sound output that indicates the specific monetary value. This functionality allows visually impaired users to effortlessly access information about the denomination of the note through voice commands

Voice access

It incorporates a wide range of powerful features, including object detection, currency detection, face recognition, and text reading capabilities. These features can be accessed through voice inputs, providing users with comprehensive information about their surroundings. Furthermore, the integration of Chat GPT allows for answering complex questions and providing detailed assistance. ​The user can also run google maps in background to navigate.

FACE RECOGNITION

To identify the name of a person standing ahead by simply pointing the camera towards them. Utilizing advanced face recognition technology, the system accurately detects and recognizes faces, and generates a voice output that announces the name of the person. This feature enables visually impaired users to easily identify and interact with individuals around them, enhancing social interactions and facilitating smoother communication
